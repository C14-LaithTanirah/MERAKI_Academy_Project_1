Game rules

  • The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  • You are X, your friend (or the computer) is O . Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  • The winner is the first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally).
  • When 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
